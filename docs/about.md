# About Afghan Tour

> The trip your friends won't believe

Afghanistan is one of the last places on earth where the word 'adventure' still means something. Afghan Tour runs small-group expeditions through the Tora Bora cave complex, the Bamyan Valley Buddha cliffs, and remote passes of the Hindu Kush. Each 10-day trip is priced at $5,000 all-inclusive, capped at 12 travelers, and run with a professional local security team and experienced expedition guides. Spring 2026 dates are now open.
