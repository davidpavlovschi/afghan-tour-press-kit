# Overview of Afghan Tour

> The trip your friends won't believe

Afghan Tour organizes guided adventure expeditions through Afghanistan's most legendary and rarely-visited locations. Our 10-day itineraries cover the Tora Bora cave complex, the Buddha ruins of Bamyan Valley, and remote Hindu Kush mountain passes. Every trip includes professional security, experienced local guides, and all-inclusive pricing at $5,000 per traveler. Groups are capped at 12 people to keep things nimble and safe. Spring 2026 departures are now accepting bookings.
